üõ†Ô∏è Tradespeople without borders: Some flexibility is coming for regulated tradespeople who want to take on work in other parts of Atlantic Canada and for those who want to work on the Island. The four Atlantic provinces have agreed to enable tradespeople to work throughout the region as efficiently and effectively as possible. An order in council from May 30 authorized Premier Dennis King and Housing Minister Rob Lantz to enter into an agreement with our neighbouring provinces to harmonize the technical safety requirements within Atlantic Canada to make this change possible.
