CAPITAL

Property owners land sweet deal

Charlottetown grew by 1 per cent last week after the city's plan to annex a portion of Marshfield was approved by the Island Regulatory and Appeals Commission.

The annexed area, which totals 116 acres, is located between Route 25 (York Road) and M&M Furniture. 

Two property owners approached the city in 2018 with a plan to develop their farm land with a mix of commercial and retail, but required a connection to the city‚Äôs water and sewer utility in order for the project to be feasible.

The city liked the plan enough that its willing to spend upward of $1M to extend utilities into the annexed area, in addition to providing police and fire protection coverage. That's a pretty sweet deal for the landowners involved.
