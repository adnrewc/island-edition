üò∑ Emergency Leave / Special Leave Fund: Islanders who fall ill to a respiratory-transmitted disease will receive some protection from their employer under modified regulations taking force this weekend. Under the modified Emergency Leave Regulations, employees diagnosed with an eligible disease, such as COVID-19 or influenza, will be protected from being fired for taking time off work, but only if a doctor or nurse practitioner advises them to stay home. The province also extended the COVID-19 Special Leave Fund until March 31. The fund, among other things, reimburses employers who continue to pay staff who take time off to recover from an illness.
