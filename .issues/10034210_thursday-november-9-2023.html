Displaced left proposed for intersection to Hillsborough Bridge

Changes are in the pipeline for the area near the Charlottetown side of the Hillsborough Bridge, with plans for a displaced left turn intersection and the realignment of Water Street. This area of the city, referred to as the Eastern Gateway (plan), has been earmarked for redevelopment for over a decade. 

The city will kickstart that redevelopment this week by starting preliminary work to rebuild Grafton Street. That work, which will ramp up next spring, will involve upgrading underground utilities between Holland College and the bridge and preparing the street for a future project to realign Water Street, connecting it with Grafton Street near Joe Ghiz Park. 

For the province‚Äôs part, it hosted an information session Wednesday evening to unveil its plan to construct a displaced left turn intersection leading to the bridge. Work on that project set to commence next summer.
